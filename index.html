2. Connect

Get them into private conversation and raise their interest

Connect

We know by fact that InMail is NOT the right approach to contact Your target audience. Why? Because it is considered as Spam or Advertisement by LinkedIn users and gets deleted by most people before even reading it! LeadGENI is different.
SCIENCE IS MAGIC THAT WORKS! The LeadGENI approach underlies a scientific study. We use pure psychology to personalize messaging, create similarities with Your lead and enhance Your perceived expertise. By doing so, we take out the distance between You and Your leads and make them feel to miss out if they don’t connect with You!

3. Pitch

Placement of your intention as a personalized soft pitch

Pitch

Once someone connected, it is time for Your pitch. The acceptance is tremendously higher compared to InMail. Why? Because we gave people the power to avoid our message. They connected with us anyways and we even teased our topic. Therefore, them connecting gives us a legit reason to pitch.
On top of that, we are experts to customize Your pitch in a “LinkedIn appropriate manner”. What we mean by that? LinkedIn is a people-to-people channel, not business-to-people. Therefore, Your pitch needs to be both specific and charming. We consult You how to do that and we use the data of hundreds of campaigns to adjust it for Your vertical.

4. Close

We make Leads book Demos with your Sales Team

Close

The final step. We don’t close the leads for You. That’s the job of Your Sales team. But we help You with everything we can to get You Calls & Meetings. We can integrate Your YouCanBook.me or Calendly into the process, create an interface with Your CRM to follow-up when requested and share best practices how to transfer a lead from a private LinkedIn conversation into Your Business environment.
